Bryant Miano

Pianist, Music Director, & Vocal Coach

Biography

Bryant Miano started his classical piano studies when he was 9 in Yokosuka, Japan. After moving to Jacksonville, FL in 2001, he graduated from Douglas Anderson School of the Arts in Piano performance under the direction of Vera Watson. During his time in high school, he auditioned and played for the pit orchestra for the school musical all 4 years.

After graduation, Bryant continued serving as an accompanist in the Jacksonville and North Florida area and started music direction musical productions in the area. In addition, he joined the Jazz Studies program at the University of the North Florida to develop his improvisational and sight-reading skills under jazz pianist Kevin Bales.

Since then, Bryant has music directed and performed in numerous musical productions.
